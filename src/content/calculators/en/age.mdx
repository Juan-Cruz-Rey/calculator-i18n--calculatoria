## Understanding Your Age Calculation

The **Age Calculator** is a precise tool that calculates your exact age from your date of birth to today or any specific date. It displays your age in multiple units: years, months, weeks, days, hours, and even minutes.

### Why Calculate Your Exact Age?

Calculating your exact age is useful for:
- **Official Documents** - Accurate age reporting for forms and applications
- **Health Purposes** - Age-related medical assessments and checkups
- **Event Planning** - Calculating anniversaries and milestones
- **Curiosity** - Discover exactly how many days or hours you've lived

### How to Use the Age Calculator

Simply enter your date of birth and the calculator will instantly display your exact age in multiple formats. You can also specify a future date to calculate how old you'll be on that day.
